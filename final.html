The discipline of Chemical Engineering has developed over the last Century to meet the needs to analyze and design processes for the large scale chemical, petroleum and biochemical industries.


The discussion on evolving trends in separation technologies and its application has aided in collaborative research and novel ideas for a more sophisticated and efficient technology towards separation processes.

ABOUT VITclose

VIT University was founded in 1984 by the name of Vellore Engineering College as a self-financing institution aimed towards building the engineering minds of the country and providing quality higher education on par with international standards The Union Ministry of Human Resources Development conferred University status on Vellore Engineering College in 2001.

Since then the university has grown from strength to strength and is now among the top educational institutions of the country.

The University’s motto “A PLACE TO LEARN, A CHANCE TO GROW” signifies how the University constantly aims towards providing quality education to its students along with the overall development of the body and mind. Students from all corners of the globe ensure a cosmopolitan atmosphere in the campus along with an international exposure to the students. A 360 acre eco-friendly campus with well-developed infrastructure and laboratories and an experienced faculty provide a technical freedom to the students.

The Institution offers 19 Undergraduate, 34 Post Graduate, 2 Integrated and 4 Research Programmes. The University is headed by its founder and chancellor, Dr. G Viswanathan , a former Parliamentarian and Minister in the Tamil Nadu Government. Sankar Viswanathan, Sekar Viswanathan and G.V. Selvam are the Vice-Presidents; Dr. Anand A. Samuel is the Vice-Chancellor and Dr. V Raju and Dr. S. Narayanan are the Pro-Vice-Chancellors.

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE



An International Conference on Separation Technologies for the Chemical, Petroleum and Biochemical Industries is meticulously planned to gather ideas from Researchers, Scientists and students from all over the country. This Two-Day Conference at the VIT University Vellore Campus during the month of October 2016 will act as a leading forum of Science and Engineering community for the presentation of new ideas which can contribute towards the advancement of this technology and act as a catalyst for the growth of Chemical Engineering as a whole.
